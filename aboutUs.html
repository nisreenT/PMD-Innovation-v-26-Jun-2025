About Us

Company profile

Pharma, Medical, Dental


PMD Innovation, established in 2025, is a forward-looking company committed to delivering top-tier pharmaceutical, medical, and dental equipment and supplies to healthcare professionals both in Syria and internationally. Our team is driven by a passion for improving healthcare outcomes by empowering medical, dental, and pharmaceutical professionals with advanced, trusted solutions. We are committed to making a positive impact on healthcare delivery by providing timely, cost-effective, and innovative products that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our vision

To revolutionize healthcare in Syria by delivering high-quality, reliable medical and dental equipment, supplies, and pharmaceuticals that empower healthcare professionals to provide exceptional care, while fostering innovation, sustainability, and trust within the field.

Our Mission

To lead the way in medical, dental, and pharmaceutical innovation by providing cutting- edge solutions that empower healthcare professionals to deliver exceptional care. Through dedication to quality, reliability, and customer service, we aim to improve patient outcomes and contribute to the continuous advancement of healthcare in Syria.

Our Commitment

At PMD Innovation, we believe that every healthcare professional should have access to the best tools and resources to provide exceptional care. Our commitment is to serve as a trusted partner in healthcare by offering products that not only meet today’s needs but also anticipate tomorrow’s challenges.

Why Choose Us?

Innovation at Our Core

We prioritize innovative solutions, ensuring that the products we offer are at the forefront of medical technology and industry trends.

Quality & Reliability

We provide only the highest-quality products, rigorously tested to meet international standards.

Customer-Focused

Our team is committed to building long-term relationships with healthcare providers, offering tailored solutions and excellent customer support.

Sustainability & Integrity

We are dedicated to upholding the highest ethical standards, ensuring responsible business practices and contributing to the sustainable development of the healthcare sector in Syria.
